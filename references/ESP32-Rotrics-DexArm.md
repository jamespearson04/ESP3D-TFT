## Specs
* ESP32
* PSRAM: 8MB   
* FLASH: 8MB
* Micro-SD card slot (SDIO 1 bit)
* 3.5-inch display with 480 x 320 ILI9488 (SPI)   
* SPI capacitive touch panel XTP2048 (SPI)    
* Built-in microphone   
* Speaker
* 1 USB-C (Serial 1)
* 1 debug port: GND, 5V, EN, GPIO 0, GPIO 2, GPIO 1 (TX), GPIO 3 (RX)
* Power Supply: 5V / 1A
